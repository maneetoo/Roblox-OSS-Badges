# Contributing
We 💖 contributions! Whether you want to add a new badge, improve an existing one, or fix a typo — **all contributions are welcome!**


## What We're Looking For
1. New badges for Roblox tools, libraries, or concepts
2. Improved designs that better match the Roblox aesthetic
3. Better documentation or examples

Do you have something similar or an idea for a Badge? We'd love to hear from you! Open an Issue or a PL
